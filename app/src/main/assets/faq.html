FAQ's

Q: How do you screen your doctors?
A: Each of our Medical Doctors are licensed to practice within their region. To become part of Doctor Pocket each medical professional must go through a strict application process and meet specific guidelines.

Q: What makes the Doctor Pocket different?
A: Doctor Pocket is the only telemedicine application that provides a network of Global Specialists of choice, at your convenience, with no time limit on your consult.

Q: How do I book an appointment?
A: To get started follow the 3 simple steps found under. How it Works?

Q: How much does it cost?
A: It's an average of $50 to book a virtual consult with one of our physicians.

Q: How do I speak to the doctor?
A: You choose the doctor you'd like to speak to based on there specialty, practice location and other details we provide. Then you pick the date and time you'd like to speak to a doctor. Then you pay. As simple as that. Instead of waiting weeks for an appointment, you can book one in 3 simple steps and talk to a doctor within 24 hours.

Q: What happens if I miss my appointment?
A: You can't. Unlike all the other telemedicine apps, on Doctor Pocket there is no time limit for your consult. Just make sure your notifications are on and you'll receive notifications once your consult starts and when your doctor answers.


Q: When it's appointment time, how do I talk to my doctor?
A: When it is time for your scheduled appointment you'll be notified. You'll also be notified whenever the doctor responds. The virtual consult is taken place on the app itself, you can send voice notes, pictures, videos and chat in real time till you're satisfied.
